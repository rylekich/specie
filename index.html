SPECIE is a design studio run by Kyle Richardson in New York City. Kyle holds a BFA from Cooper Union and MFA in Graphic Design from Yale School of Art. With a focus on special projects, SPECIE develops objects, wearables, experiential collateral, identities, websites, publications, and typefaces. SPECIE is materially promiscuous and interested in publishing in the expanded sense.

SPECIE is open for projects starting in Q3. Inquire at richtextdoc000@gmail.com. Follow on Instagram.
